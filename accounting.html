Project Overview

This project is a complete accounting implementation built in Tally ERP 9 for Sako&Saru Traders, a herbal products retailer based in Virudhunagar, Tamil Nadu, dealing in items like neem leaf, basil leaf, guava leaf, and other herbal products. Rather than custom-built software, the deliverable here is a fully configured, GST-compliant accounting system set up end-to-end inside Tally — from company creation through to final financial statements.

I built this to get hands-on, practical experience with how a real small business manages its books using industry-standard accounting software. It covers the full cycle: setting up the company profile, enabling GST, creating inventory structures for the shop's herbal products, recording day-to-day sales and purchase transactions, and generating the financial reports a business owner or auditor would actually need.

It's directly relevant to small and medium retail businesses that need accurate, GST-compliant bookkeeping without investing in a large enterprise ERP system — Tally ERP 9 remains one of the most widely used accounting platforms for exactly this kind of business in India.

Problem Statement

Small retail businesses like Sako&Saru Traders traditionally maintained accounts manually — recording sales, purchases, and expenses by hand in ledgers. This approach is slow, error-prone, and makes it difficult to produce accurate financial statements when they're needed, whether for tax filing, loan applications, or simply understanding how the business is performing.

GST compliance adds another layer of difficulty: every sale and purchase needs the correct tax rate applied and recorded separately as CGST and SGST, and errors here can lead to compliance issues. Manually tracking inventory alongside accounts — knowing exactly how much stock of each herbal product remains and what it's worth — is even harder to keep accurate over time.

Computerizing the accounting process with Tally ERP 9 addresses all of this directly: transactions are recorded once through structured vouchers, GST is calculated automatically, stock levels update in real time, and financial reports like the Trial Balance, Profit & Loss Account, and Balance Sheet can be generated instantly rather than compiled by hand.

Implementation

01

Company Setup

Created the Sako&Saru Traders company profile in Tally ERP 9 with full mailing details, financial year, and base currency information.

02

GST Configuration

Enabled Goods and Services Tax under Statutory & Taxation features and configured the applicable GSTIN, registration type, and tax rates.

03

Inventory Setup

Created stock groups (such as "Leaves") and individual stock items for each herbal product, each with its own unit of measure and GST classification.

04

Ledger Creation

Set up ledger accounts for cash, sales, purchases, and expenses to correctly classify every transaction recorded in the system.

05

Voucher Entry & Reporting

Recorded sales and purchase vouchers with GST applied, then generated the Trial Balance, Profit & Loss Account, and Balance Sheet from the accumulated data.

Challenges Faced

🧾 GST Rate Accuracy

Applying the correct CGST and SGST rates to each herbal product category required careful attention to tax classification rules.

📦 Stock Valuation

Keeping opening balances, purchases, and sales quantities consistent so closing stock values matched real inventory.

📒 Ledger Reconciliation

Ensuring every voucher entry posted to the correct ledger so the Trial Balance would tally without discrepancies.

🧮 Voucher Formatting

Learning the correct voucher type and entry format for each transaction type — sales, purchase, payment, and receipt.
